# Operation: Stink Bug
## Situation
OP-FOR has recently moved into key positions around the Province of **Chernarus** to secure strategic locations and gather intelligence. The situation is tense, as OP-FOR seeks to gain control over critical areas while maintaining a low profile.
## Mission
`A-2-0` is to establish Radio Communication coverage for future operation. There will be a covert recon team, Call-sign `Notebook`, to give intel about objectives as they come across any. They are communicating over `LoRa-SEC`, Command will be able to relay information through.
## Assists
Command has assigned an indirect fire team, Call-sign `Leapord`, to assist against heavy fire. Section Lead to give a check-in to inform them that they will be needed for the mission. All team members are to be accounted for and equipped before SL gives their check-in to `Leapord`. The section lead is to emphasize readiness and confirm communication equipment functionality.
